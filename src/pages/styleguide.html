Technology That Enables

The Essential Accessibility app is free assistive technology that helps people who have trouble typing, moving a mouse or reading a screen, navigate the web hands free and more.

The App

The Essential Accessibility app provides a suite of keyboard and mouse replacement solutions that allow people to navigate the web hands free. The app features intuitive tools such as an onscreen keyboard, face tracking and voice control. Those with limited use of their hands due to conditions such as paralysis, multiple sclerosis, arthritis or age-related factors can take advantage of everything that the web has to offer using the app.

The app is free to the end user. Organizations aiming to empower and engage people with disabilities feature the app on their websites, making it available for download—free of charge to the people who need it most. See our list of coalition members.

"Over the past 30 years, it has been our mission to support communities across the globe and it was immediately clear that [Essential Accessibility] was the next step for us in continuing on our path of driving positive social impact and helping people in need."

– Good360
